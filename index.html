ABOUT US

Medicell is pharma distribution house which provides services as C & F agents , consignee agents and superstockists catering to the state of Madhya Pradesh . We have our additional place of business at jabalpur also from where we offer our services as C & F agents , consignee agents and superstockists catering to eastern part of Madhya Pradesh. Our Indore office cum warehouse is located on A.B. road which is the hub of distribution activity for Madhya Pradesh.

We have a very excellent rapo with stockists and wholesalers in Madhya Pradesh and ensure to give zero complaint services to stockists as dispatches , billings and claims are concerned. We enjo a very high acceptibility among stockists as a very proffesional C & F agent / distributor , giving prompt , fast and error free services.

In Indore and Jabalpur we handle a turnover of 250 cr/annum. We aim at achieving 400 cr in the next 2 years.

We have our own warehouse measuring approximate 45,000 sq. ft. with excellent storage facilities and other infrastructure. The warehouses have polished kotah floor and is equipped with three goods lift.

We recieve orders upto 3 pm which are then processed , packed and handed over to the transport on the same day. The maximum time for delivery around indore is one to two days and three to four days in other parts of Madhya Pradesh.

We have been awarded four times by M/s Lupin Ltd. as the Best C & F agents of western region.
