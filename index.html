About Us

Medicell is pharma distribution house which provides services as C & F agents , consignee agents and superstockists catering to the state of Madhya Pradesh . We have our additional place of business at jabalpur also from where we offer our services as C & F agents , consignee agents and superstockists catering to eastern part of Madhya Pradesh. Our Indore office cum warehouse is located on A.B. road with easy access.

We have our own warehouse measuring approximate 45,000 sq. ft. with excellent storage facilities and other infrastructure. The warehouses have polished kotah floor and is equipped with three goods lift.

We have been awarded four times by M/s Lupin Ltd. as the Best C & F agents of western region.
